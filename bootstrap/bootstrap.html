Research Hackathon

This is an initiative by International Relations Cell, IIT Kharagpur. We aim to bring together passionate young minds across the world under a common roof where they work on pressing issues worldwide and come up with a solution which is possible to achieve with the current technologies and equipments the world possesses. These solutions are reviewed by proffessors expert in these fields from out institutes and the final selected list of candidates get a chance to visit IIT Kharagpur. These they get a lot of exposure as all the selected proposals are discussed and nurtured in the campus.
